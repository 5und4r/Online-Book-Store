Welcome to the AI Bookstore

Finding Your Next Read

Whether you know exactly what you want or are just browsing, finding books here is easy!

The Search Bar

Use the search bar at the top of the screen to find specific titles, authors, or topics.

Explore Categories

On the home page, click any of the category buttons (like 'Sci-Fi' or 'Romance') to instantly load the top-rated books in that genre. You can even sort them by length or publication year!

Book Details

Click on any book cover to open its details. You'll see the synopsis, ratings, page count, and its price.

Experience the Magic

We use cutting-edge technology to make finding and enjoying books a completely unique experience.

The AI Librarian

Click "Ask AI Librarian" at the top to chat with our virtual book expert. You can type exactly what you're in the mood for, or click the Microphone icon to use your voice! The AI will chat with you and recommend a custom list of books based on your vibe.

The Reading Experience (Atmosphere)

Whenever you click on a book to view its details, our AI works behind the scenes to analyze the plot. It will automatically generate the perfect "Reading Experience" for you—suggesting a musical soundtrack, a snack pairing, and the ideal physical setting to enjoy the story.

Blind Date with a Book

Feeling adventurous? Click "Blind Date" in the navigation bar. We will wrap a random book in mystery paper, hide the title, and only give you the 'vibes'. If you like what you read, you can unwrap it!

Managing Your Collection

To save and buy books, you will need to create a free account. Once logged in, your Vault is your personal library.

Saving for Later

Not ready to buy? Click the "Save" button on any book to add it to the Wishlist section of your Vault so you never lose track of it.

Purchasing Books

Click "Buy Now" on a book to head to our secure checkout. Once purchased, the book gets permanently moved to the "Purchased" section of your Vault, and its status will update across the entire website so you know you own it.

Read Now

Any book you have purchased will display a "Read Now" button. Clicking this instantly opens a high-quality digital reader in a new tab so you can begin reading immediately!
